Discover many aspects of school life, which result in happy, active and academically outstanding children.

Set up in 2009, DPS STS School Dhaka has become one of the most respected and academicallyacclaimed schools in Bangladesh. The school follows the Cambridge International curriculum fromPrimary to Grade 12. It is spread over two campuses (2.21 acres), one dedicated to junior sectionsand the other housing the senior sections.
